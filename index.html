    Base Rules

    Immunity toggle

    New players are initially protected from attacks.

    Killer stays permanent

    A player who becomes a killer remains a killer even if their score drops.

    Eliminate on exact zero

    Players are eliminated only if their score reaches exactly zero.

    Recovery before own turn

    A player gets one more chance to save themselves before they actually get eliminated.

    Apply modifier every turn

    If disabled, one modifier stays active for the whole round.

    Chaos Modifiers :

    Double Trouble

    Special double-based chaos effect can appear.

    Triple Trouble

    Special triple-based chaos effect can appear.

    Bonus Darts

    Players may receive extra darts during chaos rounds.

    Immunity Off

    Immunity can temporarily be disabled.

    Target Lock

    Players may be forced to hit a locked target.

    No Miss

    Missing can trigger a chaos penalty.

    Last Dart Pressure

    The final dart of a turn becomes extra risky.

    Double Damage

    Some chaos rounds deal increased damage.

    One Shot

    Players only receive 1 dart per turn.

    Safe Zone

    A protected zone can appear during chaos.

    Hot Streak

    Successful hits can chain into stronger effects.

    Revival

    One eliminated player can return to the game.

    Instant Kill

    One dart only. A valid hit from a killer eliminates instantly.

    Sudden Death

    All alive players drop to 1 score.

    Bullseye Madness

    Normal hits score 1. Outer Bull scores 2 and Inner Bull scores 3. Doubles and triples count as 1.

    First Blood

    Your first dart of the turn gives +1 extra point if it hits.

    Random Target Swap

    Each player gets a temporary swapped target number.

    Equalizer

    Players with the lowest score gain +1 extra point on their first successful hit this round.

    Focus

    Hit all three darts in your turn to gain +1 extra point on the last hit.

    Open Season

    All players can attack any alive player this round.

    Steady Hand

    Scores cannot drop below 0 while a killer is active.

    Lucky Hit

    Each successful hit has a 25% chance to grant +1 extra point.

    Vampire Mode

    Hits can steal value back for the attacker.
